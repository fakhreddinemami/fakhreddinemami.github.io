Developed large-scale scientific machine learning surrogates, including shared-weight MLPs and graph neural networks, trained on synthetic FEA datasets. The workflow integrated uncertainty quantification and reduced selected full FEA workflows from about 300 CPU-hours to minutes with GPU-accelerated inference.
